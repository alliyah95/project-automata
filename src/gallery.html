Project Automata

Before the actuality of Project Automata, it was nothing more than just an idea on a piece of paper. An idea that was refined continually, then eventually attained the ideal design of the product which is an automated script that improves the overall performance of slow computers through the process of file clean-up, protection, and restoration.
